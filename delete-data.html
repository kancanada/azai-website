Request Data Deletion

AZAI Oracle collects minimal, anonymous data. We do not collect any personal information such as names, emails, or account details. The only data we may collect is anonymous usage analytics (which stock tickers were searched).

To request deletion of your data:

  1. Send an email to asoni@kancanada.com
  2. Include the subject line: "Data Deletion Request - AZAI Oracle"
  3. We will process your request and delete any associated data within 30 days

Since AZAI Oracle does not require account creation and does not collect personal identifiers, most users have no personally identifiable data stored in our systems.
