Frequently Asked Questions

What is AZAI Oracle?

AZAI Oracle is an AI-powered investment analysis app that helps you evaluate stocks and ETFs. It uses a Discounted Earnings Model to calculate intrinsic values and an AI engine to provide detailed analysis.

How is the intrinsic value calculated?

We use an EPS-based Discounted Earnings Model with a 12% discount rate, 4% terminal growth rate, and a 20% maximum growth cap. This gives you a fair value estimate to compare against the current market price.

How are ETFs rated?

ETFs are scored using a 5-factor composite model: Cost Efficiency (25%), Performance (25%), Yield (20%), Size (15%), and Diversification (15%). Each factor is scored 0-100 and combined into an overall rating.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. AZAI Oracle does not collect any personal information. No login is required, and no personal data leaves your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Why does a stock show "Analysis Failed"?

This can happen if the stock has insufficient financial data on Yahoo Finance, the ticker symbol is incorrect, or if there's a temporary network issue. Try again in a few minutes.
