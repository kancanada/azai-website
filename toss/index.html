How it works
01

Install on both devices

Drag Toss.app into Applications. Install AZAI Toss from Google Play on your phone. Open each once.

02

Send

On Mac: drag a file onto the window, or right-click in Finder → Share → Toss. On phone: any file → Share → Toss → pick the Mac.

03

Receive

The other device shows an Accept prompt. Approved files land in ~/Downloads/Toss/ on Mac and in the Gallery / Downloads on phone.

Built differently
01

No cloud

Files go directly between your devices over local Wi-Fi. Nothing touches our servers because we don't have any.

02

End-to-end encrypted

XChaCha20-Poly1305 per chunk. Mutual identity authentication. Forward secrecy on every session.

03

Pay once

Free up to 1 GB per transfer. $29 one-time unlock for larger files. No subscription, no upsell.
