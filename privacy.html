Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

AZAI Oracle ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

AZAI Oracle is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Usage Analytics (Anonymous): We may collect anonymous data about which stock and ETF tickers are searched within the app. This data is not linked to any personal identity and is used solely to improve the app experience (e.g., identifying popular stocks, improving coverage).
  • Feedback Data (Optional): If you voluntarily submit feedback using the in-app feedback feature, we collect the ticker symbol, your feedback rating, and any optional comment you provide. No personal information is collected with feedback submissions.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Financial account information or trading data
  • Photos, contacts, or files from your device
  • Advertising identifiers

The app does not require account creation or login.

3. How We Use Information

Any anonymous data collected is used to:

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Identify popular stocks and ETFs to improve coverage
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance

4. Third-Party Services

AZAI Oracle uses the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

  • Yahoo Finance: To retrieve real-time stock and ETF financial data. No personal data is shared with Yahoo Finance.
  • Google Gemini AI: To generate AI-powered analysis of stocks and ETFs. Only financial data (not personal data) is sent to this service.

5. Data Storage

Anonymous usage data, if collected, is stored in a secured Google Sheets database. No personal information is stored. All financial data displayed in the app is fetched in real-time and is not stored on our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

AZAI Oracle is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
